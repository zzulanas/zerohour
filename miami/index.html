This is Zero Hour:
The Youth Climate Summit

July 12-14 2019
Miami, FL

A year after the historic youth climate march, the youth strike again! Join Zero Hour in This Is Zero Hour: The Youth Climate Summit in Miami, a frontline community. The Summit will be held from July 12 – 14 at DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Miami Airport & Convention Center, 711 NW 72nd Avenue, Miami, FL 33126.

Registration will be open soon! Subscribe to Email Updates to get notified when registration opens.

Interested in donating? Your monetary support will ensure that youth activists can participate in events and continue organizing to make sure that youth voices are heard loud and clear!

Donate or Partner

We would also love to accept in-kind donations of resources you or your organization may be able to give. For instance—houses and living spaces in Miami to host young activists for our day of action, cars and other means of transportation, resources and supplies for training and workshops we host. We promise we’ll put every dollar to good use! Please reach out to us at info@thisiszerohour.org if you want to donate any of the above items or other resources.

If you are an organization interested in partnering, please contact Arielle@thisiszerohour.org. For media inquires, contact media@thisiszerohour.org For general inquiries, contact info@thisiszerohour.org
